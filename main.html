Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city rich in culture, tradition, and stunning natural beauty. Known for its classical Buddhist temples, imperial palaces, gardens, and traditional wooden houses, Kyoto offers a peaceful escape into Japan's historic heart.

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine – Famous for its thousands of vibrant torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – A stunning temple covered in gold leaf.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – A serene walk through towering bamboo stalks.
  4. Gion District – Explore Kyoto’s geisha culture and historic streets.
  5. Philosopher’s Path – A peaceful cherry blossom-lined canal.
  • Matcha ice cream – A creamy dessert made with green tea powder.
  • Yudofu – A simple but delicious tofu hot pot.
  • Kaiseki – A traditional multi-course meal that’s both artful and seasonal.
  • Soba noodles – Buckwheat noodles served cold or hot with a flavorful broth.
  • Tsukemono – An assortment of Japanese pickles served with meals.
